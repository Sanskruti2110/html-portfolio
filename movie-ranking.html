Zara Hatke Zara Bachke

"Zara Hatke Zara Bachke" (2023) is a U/A rated drama/family film with a runtime of 2 hours and 20 minutes. While it may not be a masterpiece, it certainly deserves a one-time watch for its unique blend of sarcasm and compelling performances.
